January 25-26th, 2013

Reimbursement

How to get a reimbursements:

Please email quintin@scottylabs.org with the Subject: Reimbursements

Please include the following:

1. Your Name
2. Full Address (where we will mail the check)
3. An attached copy of your ticket
4. A description of what type of transportation you took, ie I took the Mega Bus from Philly to Pittsburgh on (date) and returned on (date).
5. Total amount of cost (proof of purchase, or receipt)

How much we'll reimburse:

TartanHacks is pleased to offer up a number of tickets this year to other schools in the Northeast. We are currently limiting registration from non-CMU schools to a select number of universities. For more information see the rules or registration

We're going to be reimbursing some if not all of your travel costs for getting into Pittsburgh and over to Carnegie Mellon.

If you can, and it's reasonable, we'll ask that you take a bus. We recommend Megabus and Greyhound. Both of these options will drop you in the downtown area of Pittsburgh. You can take a Port Authority busline (61A/61B/61C/61D lines work the best) from the downtown area up to CMU.

For bus travelers, we will reimburse up to $75. Just keep your receipts (for bus tickets, food, taxies, etc)

If buses aren't really an option, you are welcome to drive to CMU. We'll reimburse up to $125 for each person that makes the trek by car. Again, just keep your receipts together.

Finally, if these are all not an option, you are welcome to travel by air. We'll reimburse $150 for those who travel by air. From the Pittsburgh International Airport, you can take the 28X bus and ride it all the way to it's last stop, which is Carnegie Mellon.
