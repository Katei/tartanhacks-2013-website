Let's build something.

TartanHacks 2013 will begin on Friday, January 25 at 5:45pm on the 4th floor of the Gates Hillman Center at Carnegie Mellon University.

TartanHacks is a new kind of hackathon: one for people who have ideas and want to make them happen.

TartanHacks is a hackathon designed for people who have great ideas for cool things. Last year, TartanHacks became one of the biggest hackathons on CMU’s campus (and it's entirely student-run). We had an awesome time last year. See winning hacks and photos from last year.

TartanHacks 2013

The hackathon will take place on January 25-26, 2013, and registration will open in the first week of the Spring 2013 semester. Leading up to the hackathon, we’ll be hosting student-led, skill-building talks. At the hackathon, we’ll have expert mentors, great food, and, well, fun.

General registration for TartanHacks 2013 will open on January 16th. Check back on the site starting January 16th for the registration link.

Who Runs TartanHacks?

TartanHacks is hosted by ScottyLabs in association with the Carnegie Mellon University School of Computer Science Undergraduate Program, Women@SCS, and Project Olympus.
